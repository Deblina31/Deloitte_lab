List of Tourist Attractions in Kolkata

  1. Howrah Bridge

    2. An iconic cantilever bridge over the Hooghly River. It looks especially beautiful at night with lights.

  2. Victoria Memorial

    3. A grand white marble monument built in memory of Queen Victoria. It has a museum and a huge garden.

  3. Eden Gardens

    4. One of the most famous cricket stadiums in the world.

  4. Eco Park

    5. A beautiful modern park with boating, gardens, and replicas of world monuments.
